$\left(V_{1,1} \wedge V_{1,2} \wedge\left(\bigwedge\limits_{i=1}^3V_{1i,3}\right)\right)$\

$\left(V_{1,1} \wedge \neg V_{1,2} \wedge \left(\bigwedge\limits_{i=1}^3\left(V_{1i,2} \vee V_{1i,3}\right)\right)\right)$\
$\vee$\
$\left(\neg V_{1,1} \wedge V_{1,2} \wedge \left(\bigwedge\limits_{i=1}^3\left(V_{1i,1} \vee \neg V_{1i,2} \vee V_{1i,3}\right)\right)\right)$\
$\vee$\
$\left(\neg V_{1,1} \wedge \neg V_{1,2} \wedge \left(\bigwedge\limits_{i=1}^3\left(V_{1i,1} \vee V_{1i,2} \vee V_{1i,3}\right)\right)\right)$\

$V_{1,3} = \bigwedge\limits_{i=1}^3\left(V_{1i,3} \vee \left(V_{1i,2} \oplus V_{1,2}\right) \vee \left(V_{1i,1} \oplus V_{1,1}\right) \right)$\
